Assignment 2

Scouting footballers

About the dataset and usefulness

Football is one of the most popular sports in the world and is enjoyed by millions of people. One of the key factors that contribute to the success of a football team is the quality of its players. Therefore, the ability to identify and recruit talented youngsters is crucial for the long-term success of any football club. In this context, the use of data analytics and visualization tools can provide valuable insights into the performance of individual players and teams, as well as help identify patterns and trends in playing styles. The scouting dashboard created using Tableau utilizes data sourced from Sofifa, which contains information on 18,944 players. The data can be used to provide feedback to talent, identify future talent, understand patterns in the playing styles of opponents, and even for sports betting purposes. The dashboard will be a valuable tool for scouts, coaches, and football enthusiasts, enabling them to make informed decisions based on data-driven insights.



Bonus section

Analyzing trends in player heights can provide valuable insights into the physical attributes that are required for success in football. The sport is becoming increasingly physical, and height can be a decisive factor in a player's ability to compete at the highest level.

Information visualization using python plotly

About the dataset and usefulness

The dataset I used to create two bar graphs visualizing Indian prison statistics in Maharashtra from 2001 to 2013 is sourced from Kaggle, and the link to the dataset can be found here. This dataset provides valuable information about the criminal justice system in India, including details on the caste and gender of prisoners in Maharashtra during the specified period. By creating two bar graphs using this data, I was able to explore and communicate different aspects of the data, such as the overall number of prison convicts in Maharashtra and the gender disparities in the prison statistics. The usefulness of this dataset and the bar graphs I created lies in the insights they provide into the criminal justice system in Maharashtra and India as a whole. Policymakers, researchers, and citizens alike can use this information to identify areas for improvement in the criminal justice system, such as addressing gender disparities and reducing the overall number of prison convicts. The visualization can also be used to raise awareness about the issue and drive positive change in the criminal justice system.
